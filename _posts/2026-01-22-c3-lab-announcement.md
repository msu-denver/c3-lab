We're thrilled to announce that **Dr. Daniel Pittman** and **Dr. Ranjidha Rajan** have officially launched the **Community-Centered Computing Lab (C3 Lab)** in MSU Denver's Department of Computer Sciences.

The C3 Lab's mission is simple: design and build technology that empowers communities. The lab focuses on human-centered computing, applied AI, and accessible data systems that make a real difference for the people and organizations served.

Active projects in the lab include the NSF-funded [Colorado Sustainability Hub]({{ '/projects/sustainability-hub/' | relative_url }}), the SIPA-funded [RILE Connect]({{ '/projects/rile-connect/' | relative_url }}) STEM platform, the student-built [Roadrunner Connect]({{ '/projects/roadrunner-connect/' | relative_url }}) campus engagement app, and the [Peer Support Hub]({{ '/projects/peer-support-hub/' | relative_url }}) — all building production software that serves Colorado communities while giving students hands-on research and engineering experience.
