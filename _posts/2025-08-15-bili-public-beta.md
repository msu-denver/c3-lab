Bili — the AI-powered chatbot at the heart of the [Colorado Sustainability Hub]({{ '/projects/sustainability-hub/' | relative_url }}) — is now live in public beta at [sustainabilityhub.co](https://sustainabilityhub.co/).

Bili (short for *sustaina-bili-ty*) helps anyone explore complex sustainability questions in a more approachable, conversational way. The public beta is the result of nearly two years of design, engineering, and community engagement work led by MSU Denver students and faculty, with research collaborators from CSU Fort Collins, CSU Pueblo, University of Northern Colorado, and University of Denver.

This milestone marks the first time Coloradans can ask sustainability questions in plain language and get research-backed, citation-grounded answers from a single platform. The team will continue iterating on Bili's responses, its underlying data sources, and the conversational experience as community feedback comes in.
